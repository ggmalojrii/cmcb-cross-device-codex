Housekeeping Console

The housekeeping console is the safe file-management surface. It builds request packets for add, edit, delete, move, rename, and list operations inside the allowed CMCB roots. Destructive changes stay approval-gated and secret-like paths are blocked.

Open the housekeeping console or read HOUSEKEEPING_CONSOLE.md for the queue flow and safety rules.
